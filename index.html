Welcome to the Colloids and Surface at IITM

Our research group is interested in understanding the fundamental aspects of colloidal and interfacial phenomena. We are particularly interested in the following areas:

  • Investigation of particle shape effects in colloidal systems
  • Structure and rheology of colloids
  • Desiccation cracks and external fields
  • Hetero-aggregation in colloidal systems
  • New materials and particles
  • Biological and Environmental Interfaces
  • Image processing and analysis
  • Emulsion fuels

We use a combination of experimental, theoretical, and computational techniques to study these phenomena. Our research is highly interdisciplinary and involves collaborations with researchers from various disciplines.

Our research is supported by various funding agencies, including the Department of Science and Technology (DST), the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), the Council of Scientific and Industrial Research (CSIR), and the Indian National Science Academy (INSA).
