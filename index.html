Dr. Madivala G. Basavaraj

Professor, Department of Chemical Engineering, IIT Madras

Email: basa@iitm.ac.in | Phone: +91 44 22574164

Madivala G. Basavaraj is an associate professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. He obtained his Ph.D. from KU Leuven, Belgium and carried out postdoctoral research at the University of Delaware, USA. His research interests include particles at interfaces, colloid self-assembly, desiccation cracks, colloidal gels, emulsion fuels, Pickering emulsions, and cellulose–ionic liquid solutions.
