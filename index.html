Welcome to Dr. Madivala G. Basavaraj's Page

Biography

Madivala G. Basavaraj is a professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. He obtained his Ph.D. from KU Leuven, Belgium and carried out postdoctoral research at the University of Delaware, USA. His research interests include particles at interfaces, colloid self-assembly, desiccation cracks, colloidal gels, emulsion fuels, Pickering emulsions, and cellulose–ionic liquid solutions.

Research Interests

  • Evaporation Driven Self-Assembly
  • Structure and rheology of colloids
  • Desiccation cracks and external fields
  • Hetero-aggregation in colloidal systems
  • New materials and particles
  • Biological and Environmental Interfaces
  • Image processing and analysis
  • Emulsion fuels

Approach

My research employs a combination of experimental, theoretical, and computational techniques. I collaborate with researchers from various disciplines, making our work highly interdisciplinary.
